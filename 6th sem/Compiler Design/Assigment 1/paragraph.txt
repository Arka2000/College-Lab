 It was a dark, gray day but even the darkest of days can turn sunny. Depressed 
 by the weather, he put on his warmest coat. He could not find his gloves so he 
 shoved his cold hands deep into the pockets of his coat. "Hey!" he said and so
 loud his mother could hear him. "Hey what?" she called out to him from the 
 kitchen. "I found my missing locker key! he said. He happily put the key into 
 his backpack and was smiling as he walked out the door. The day was still dark
 and gray but his mood was now a sunny one.
